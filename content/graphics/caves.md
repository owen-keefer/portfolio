City of Caves

2024 - 2025

Illustration

Partnership with Nottingham University's archaeology department to document the hidden network of caves beneath the city.  Produced an illustrated map of Nottingham combining GIS data with traditional media to visualise a selection of historic cave sites.  Bridges digital cartography and hand drawn illustration to make archaelogical research accessible to a broader public audience.
